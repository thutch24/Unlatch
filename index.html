Feed Drop Roadmap

Days 1–6 build the new association. Nap feed drops by day 7.

💛 Remember: The first 3–5 nights are the hardest. Her behaviour will escalate before it improves — that is completely normal. It doesn't mean it's not working. Stay the course.
3-Week Plan

🎯 Week 1 Goal: Drop the Midday Nap Feed

Days 1–6 are about building the foundation. Day 7 is the drop. Don't skip the foundation — it makes everything easier.

🌿 Week 2 Goal: Drop the Morning Feed

Apply the same approach to mornings. Use your comfort object and ritual, unlatch while drowsy, and hold the new routine.

🌙 Week 3 Goal: Bedtime + Overnight

The hardest week, but you have momentum. Tackle bedtime and overnight together. Partner takes nights if possible.

Tips & Strategies
When She's Screaming
🗣️
Your anchor phraseSay "Milk is sleeping. Mummy is here." calmly and consistently every single time.
🫂
You can still comfort herGo to her, hold her, pat her. You are NOT abandoning her — you're just not offering the boob.
💪
Stay the courseNights 1–5 are the hardest. Behaviour escalates before it improves — that's completely normal.
Building a New Sleep Cue
🧸
Comfort objectIntroduce a small stuffed animal or blanket during feeds so it starts to feel familiar before any feeds are dropped.
🔊
White noise or shushingSame sound every single time — becomes a powerful sleep trigger. Also drowns out the crying for your own sanity at 2am. Way better than singing (especially if she's a critic 😂).
😴
Unlatch while drowsy, not asleepFeed until she's drowsy but not out. Unlatch, keep holding, keep the cue going. This is the key skill.
Overnight Strategies
👨‍👩‍👧
Partner takes nightsThe single most effective shortcut. She's less likely to escalate when the boob isn't in the room.
💧
Offer waterA sippy cup of water for night wakes gives you something to offer that isn't the boob.
📅
Gradual window methodNo feeds before 3am for a few nights, then push to 5am, then morning only. Takes 1–2 weeks but is gentler.
For Your Body
🥬
Cabbage leavesGenuinely help with engorgement. Pop in the fridge first for extra relief.
🤲
Hand express minimallyExpress just enough for comfort — not full relief. This signals your body to reduce supply.
Fussy Eating During Weaning
🍽️
Shift the dinner orderTry: dinner → bath → books → feed → sleep. The gap means food has time to register.
🧀
Healthy fats and proteinCheese, avocado, egg, meat. Don't stress about variety right now — just get calories in.
📈
Food will improve after weaningOnce the boob is less available, food becomes more interesting by default. Usually within 1–2 weeks.
You've got this 💛
18 months of breastfeeding is genuinely incredible. You are not abandoning her — you are teaching her a new way to feel safe. She is almost 18 months old. She is ready, even if she doesn't think so yet.
